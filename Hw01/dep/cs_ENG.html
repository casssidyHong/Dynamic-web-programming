The Department of Computer Science at National Chiao Tung University (NCTU), now part of National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU) following the university merger, is one of Taiwan’s leading institutions for computer science education and research. Founded in 1972, the department has a long-standing reputation for academic excellence and innovation.

The department offers a comprehensive curriculum covering core areas such as algorithms, data structures, computer architecture, software engineering, and artificial intelligence. It also provides specialized courses in emerging fields like machine learning, cloud computing, and cybersecurity, equipping students with cutting-edge knowledge and skills.

NCTU’s Computer Science program emphasizes both theoretical foundations and practical applications. Students have access to state-of-the-art research facilities, computing resources, and opportunities to participate in research projects alongside faculty members. The department maintains strong connections with industry, providing students with internships, collaborative projects, and networking opportunities that prepare them for successful careers in technology.

Graduates of the program are highly sought after by leading companies in Taiwan and around the world, with many continuing their studies in top graduate programs or launching careers in diverse fields such as software development, data science, and hardware design.

Located in the high-tech hub of Hsinchu, the department benefits from its proximity to Taiwan’s thriving semiconductor and technology industries, making it an ideal place for students to engage with the latest technological advancements and trends.
