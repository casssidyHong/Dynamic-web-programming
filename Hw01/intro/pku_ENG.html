Information

Founded in 1898, Peking University (PKU) is one of China's oldest and most prestigious institutions of higher learning. It is located in the Haidian District of Beijing, close to the Summer Palace and other historic landmarks. The university is known for its rigorous academic programs, distinguished faculty, and diverse student body.

Peking University is not only renowned for its humanities, social sciences, and natural sciences programs but also for its vibrant research environment, fostering innovation and academic excellence. The university campus, often referred to as "Yan Yuan," features traditional Chinese architecture alongside modern facilities, creating a unique blend of historical and contemporary elements.

With a global reputation and collaborations with many top universities around the world, Peking University attracts students from all over the globe, making it a hub for cultural exchange and academic discourse. It has a strong commitment to promoting knowledge and serving society through education, research, and innovation.

The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, master's, and doctoral programs, making it an ideal destination for students looking to pursue their studies in China. Moreover, Peking University is home to many renowned scholars and has produced a number of notable alumni who have made significant contributions in various fields.
