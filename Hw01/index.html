Cross-Disciplinary Program

Due to my strong interest in Electronic Engineering, I applied for the Cross-Disciplinary Program for a double major during my freshman year. The field of Electronic Engineering has always fascinated me with its focus on the design and development of electronic systems, from circuits to complex integrated systems that power modern devices.

At the university, the Electronic Engineering department offers a diverse curriculum that spans foundational topics such as circuit theory, electromagnetics, and semiconductor devices, to more advanced subjects like microelectronics, signal processing, and communication systems. Students are not only trained in theoretical knowledge but are also given practical experience through hands-on labs and projects. This combination of theory and practice is what drew me to pursue a cross-disciplinary program, as I believe the skills gained from both Computer Science and Electronic Engineering will complement each other and prepare me for future challenges in technology and innovation.

Kaohsiung Municipal Kaohsiung Girls' Senior High School

Kaohsiung Girls' Senior High School (KGHS), established in 1938, is one of the most renowned and prestigious all-girls institutions in southern Taiwan. With a rich history and strong academic tradition, the school has consistently dedicated itself to empowering young women to become well-rounded individuals who excel in various fields, including academics, arts, and sports.

KGHS is known for its strong emphasis on both intellectual growth and character development. The school fosters an environment where students are encouraged to be independent learners and critical thinkers. It offers a wide range of extracurricular activities, from student clubs to athletic teams, as well as cultural events and competitions. These opportunities allow students to cultivate their talents and interests while gaining important life skills such as leadership, teamwork, and communication.

The school places great importance on a supportive and harmonious atmosphere, where teachers and students build close and meaningful relationships. Students are motivated to challenge themselves academically and personally, striving for excellence in every aspect of their lives. KGGSH has also produced numerous successful alumni who have gone on to make significant contributions in various sectors, including science, politics, business, and the arts.

Through its commitment to academic rigor and holistic education, Kaohsiung Girls' Senior High School continues to be a leader in education, providing its students with the knowledge and skills necessary to thrive in an ever-changing world.
